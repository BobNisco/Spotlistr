Convert Anything to a Spotify Playlist

Spotlistr is a collection of tools to help you generate Spotify playlists from many different sources!

Easily convert playlists from YouTube to Spotify Playlists!

Discover music based on your Last.fm top tracks, or based on Last.fm's similar tracks!

Create Spotify playlists based on music Subreddits and Multireddits!

Convert a SoundCloud set/playlist into a Spotify playlist!

Why Spotlistr?

Spotlistr is the most feature-packed music discovery tool out there! For example, Spotlistr allows anyone to easily discover new music based on what they already listen to by just inputting their favorite tracks in our Last.fm's similar tracks search service.

Spotify playlists made easy

Creating a Spotify playlist can be daunting, and downright time consuming! Spotlistr allows you to create a playlist in just a few clicks. You'll never go back to creating Spotify playlists in any other way!
